# Interactive Weather Art
COMS BC 3930

## Materials:
- ESP32
- 1 Motor
- 1 L293D
- 1 Stepper Motor
- 1 ULN2003 Stepper Motor Driver
- 1 Photoresistor
- 1 10k Ohm Resistor
- 9V Battery <br />

** Lost count of amt of wires used

## Hardware Configuration
### Stepper Motor Driver
GND - GND <br />
VCC  - 5V <br />
Following are connected to Stepper Motor <br />
A - Blue <br />
B - Pink <br />
C  - Yellow <br />
D  - Orange <br />
PWR  - Red <br />
